Top 5 Holy Places of INDIA

Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the best places holy places in India. Varanasi is the religious capital, is located on the banks of the sacred Ganges in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh. This holy land is associated with Lord Shiva and is one of the Hindus’ seven holy sites. A plethora of temples is dedicated to Lord Shiva and other Hindu pantheon deities.The Bharat Mata Mandir, which is worshipped by people of all religions, is a must-see temple. This temple is interesting because there is no image of a deity; instead, there is a relief map of India made of marble that represents secularism. The most well-known temples in Varanasi are the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, the Durga Temple, and the Tulsi Manas Temple. Visit the many ghats—or steps leading down to the river—while in Varanasi. The most important are Dashashwamedh Ghat, Assi Ghat, and Manikarnika Ghat.

Rishikesh

The emerald Ganga, numerous Hindu temples, and dense concentration of yoga centers have reshaped Rishikesh’s spirituality. Rishikesh, by preserving the traditional Hindu practice of Yoga, provides a gateway to a deeper level of spirituality in India.Rishikesh allows you to praise nature in its purest form and lose yourself in it. As a result, this remarkable city is one of the most beautiful holy places in India.

Dwarka

Dwarka, on India’s westernmost tip (Gujarat), is a land of unparalleled mysticism. It is also known as the Gateway to Heaven and indeed if you will visit this one of the most beautiful holy places in India, you yourself will say this.After fleeing from Mathura, Lord Krishna is said to have established Dwarka as his kingdom. Worshippers from all over the world have been drawn to this fascinating city since then.Dwarkadhish Temple is a prominent Lord Krishna shrine in Dwarka. The simple act of visiting this celestial shrine brings you one step closer to Nirvana. The temple yatra of Dwarkadhish is an important part of the heavenly Char Dham Yatra. The white dress of the local men adds to the solemnity of the setting. Dwarka is a beautiful town that will transport you back in time to the time of the Mahabharata. Guggali Brahmin changes the 52-yard flag of Dwarkadhish temple five times a day, three times in the morning and twice in the evening. To achieve eternal peace, one must visit Dwarkadhish’s divine temple.

Haridwar

Here comes the next most fantastic holy place in India-Haridwar. Haridwar in the mythical Uttarakhand is nothing short of an empyrean for Hindus. The sacred village, located on the banks of the holy river Ganga, exudes a profound sense of calm. This is the sole reason why Haridwar is said to be one of the most serene holy places in India.When you are in Haridwar you can see the glowing Diyas floating down the dazzling river in the evening, along with the energizing Ganga Aarti. Har ki Pauri is one of the best places to visit in Haridwar if you’re looking to seek unrivaled holiness here. The Kumbh Mela is also one of the most & best-visited places. It takes place every 12 years that attracts millions of visitors; however, devotees visit the city all year.

Rameshwaram

Rameshwaram, in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu, is one of the stops on the holy Char Dham yatra. This sacred site on the island of Mannar is believed to be the location where Lord Rama prayed and repented after murdering Raavana. Rameshwaram is indeed one of the best holy places in India. Rameshwaram is steeped in spirituality, and the Ramanathaswamy temple is the best place to immerse yourself in it.This magnificent temple is known for its magnificent architecture that blends divinity and culture.
