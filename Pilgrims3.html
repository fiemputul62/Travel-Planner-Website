Nageshwar Jyotirlinga

Believed to be the first amongst the 12 powerful Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva, the Nageshwar Jyotirlinga is one of the most prominent religious places to visit in Dwarka. The scared shrine complex also houses a majestic 80-feet Shiva statue inspiring awe.

Sudama Setu

This small and serene temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is one of the must-visit places in Dwarka for its scenic setting and tranquil vibes. Overlooking the Arabian Sea, the temple is surrounded by blue waters, golden sand, and a calming breeze all day long. The evening aarti at the temple, with the sound of the crashing waves, is a humbling and soothing experience.
