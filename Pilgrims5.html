Holy Places of RAMESHWARAM

Ramanathaswamy Temple

An extremely popular Place to Visit in Rameshwaram, Ramanathaswamy Temple forms the most significant part of Rameshwaram Tourism. Famous for being one of the 12 revered sites of Jyotirlingas. Pilgrims visit this beautiful Rameshwaram Temples to pray to the Hindu God, Lord Shiva, established here in the form of Jyotirlinga.

Built to pay respects to Lord Lakshmana the younger brother of Lord Rama, Lakshmana Theertham is a popular Tourist Place in Rameshwaram which is also a dedicated sanctum of Lord Shiva. The temple was constructed here so as to commemorate the Hindu deities and offer them prayers.
